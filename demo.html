AIDEC™ — Decision System Demo

This page demonstrates how AIDEC™ visualizes decisions. The graphs below are not analytics dashboards. They are **decision-clarity tools**.

1. Decision Funnel

Every input is filtered through structured stages. Most decisions should never reach execution.

Purpose: Show decision reduction, not conversion.

2. Risk vs Impact Matrix

AIDEC™ forces trade-off visibility. High-impact decisions with unmanaged risk are blocked.

Purpose: Expose hidden risk, not predict outcome.

3. Cost vs Delay Trade-Off

Decisions are evaluated against financial cost and execution delay. Cheap but slow decisions are flagged.

Purpose: Make consequences explicit before execution.
